Wiring decisions finalized
--------------------------
1. LCD (I2C): SDA->A4, SCL->A5, VCC->5V, GND->GND
2. pH module: V+->5V, G->GND, Po->A0
   - Do optional (threshold digital), To ignored (no temp output probe)
3. TDS module: A->A1, VCC->5V, GND->GND
4. DHT11: DATA->D2 (NOT A2), VCC->5V, GND->GND


Behavior notes discussed
------------------------
1. Uncalibrated pH probe + cap on => fluctuating raw values are expected.
2. pH probe should be conditioned/stored wet (KCl/storage solution).
3. For safe soil testing: use flowable slurry/supernatant, avoid mechanical damage to bulb.
4. SIM mode should be used until final calibration for meaningful pH values.
